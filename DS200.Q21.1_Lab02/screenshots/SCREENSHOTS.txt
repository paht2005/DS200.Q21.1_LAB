Screenshots for submission (include your OS username in the capture)
======================================================================

Course submissions often require proof that you ran the jobs yourself. Capture
your terminal or desktop in a way that shows:

1) Your username (examples: run `whoami` on macOS/Linux, or show your prompt).
2) The command you ran (Pig local run or the Python mirror).
3) A snippet of printed paths or `head` of a result file under output/.

Suggested commands (from the Lab02 root folder):

  whoami
  date
  bash scripts/run_pig_local.sh
  # or, if Pig is not installed:
  python3 scripts/generate_outputs.py
  head -n 10 output/task03_summary.tsv

Save images as PNG or JPG in this folder, for example:

  screenshots/01_whoami_and_run.png
  screenshots/02_task03_summary_head.png

You can remove this instruction file before pushing if your course only wants images.
